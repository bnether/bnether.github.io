Biodegradable PolyNatural® Foliage Clip

Date: September 2023 – November 2023
Client: EPL Group Ltd


Optimised the geometry and material selection of a biodegradable foliage clip for minimum cost, to meet existing load requirements.

  • Finite element analysis was performed on an existing polypropylene foliage clip and verified using tensile tests. This was to validate the model before proceeding.
  • The same geometry was then tested using seven biodegradable compounds, indicating the most suitable material to progress with.
  • Various geometric designs using this material were simulated with FEA, and a topology optimization task was run to mathematically generate an optimum design.
  • Properties were finalised such as maximum loads, stress concentrations, factors of safety, material volume and cost savings.


The foliage clip has since been released to market, and can be purchased here: PolyNatural
